UID Climate Group

The UID Climate Group is a student initiative at Umeå Institute of Design. By uniting students from all programs it aims to cultivate the immense potential within design to work for a sustainable future. Consequently, we aspire to be a part of the sustainable transformation by applying a more conscious approach to design and education. By working collectively and interdisciplinary with other faculties during this project it has been possible to explore a problem from different angles, to, in the end, understand the topic on a wider scale. The plant wall aims to raise awareness among students and the local society about the importance of boreal forests and explore ways to bridge the percieved human-nature gap in the physical space.

The project was also a practise for us to work with many stakeholders, and we hope to keep facilitating projects like this, keep learning, and strive to make us and our classmates designers with the health of our planet and its inhabitants as a foundation of our future design careers.
