(what)

Growing out of Växtvägg, the Dome enables other living beings to be within a plant wall.

(why)

The structure explodes the plantwall into three dimensionalspace, to take it beyond flat decoration. By giving a plantwall space and form, the Dome invites visitors to sit quietly together, and spend time close to the wall and one another. It connects students and visitors at the UmU Arts Campus, and invites them to form a stronger bond with the wall, each other and, we hope, with nature.


(how)

The main inspiration for the Dome is "The Growroom", an open source project from the design and research lab Space 10. We adapted their design to our space, using procedural modeling to prototype the visual appearance from a sculpted shape. This method allowed us to iterate many possibilities and output a high fidelity visual reference to gather feedback. The next step for this project is to build a full scale model and evaluate how well the design can create the desired effect.
